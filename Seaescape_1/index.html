Sea Escape

Our History

SeaEscape is perched on a cliff that rises 180 feet from the ocean and affords spectacular, oceanfront views of the rugged northern California coast. Located in Elk, Mendocino County, California, SeaEscape is just 20 minutes south of Mendocino. Local activities include coastal sightseeing, shopping, ocean kayaking, fishing, whale watching, horseback riding, local wineries, hiking and many other actives. Enjoy whitewater views from your private deck and Hot Tub. This is lodging at its best!


Features During Your Stay
