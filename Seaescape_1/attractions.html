Directions & Attractions

Sea Escape is located at 7426 South Highway One, Elk, CA 95432

Highway One’s reputation for beautiful scenery excels in Mendocino County. SeaEscape is about 160 miles north of San Francisco, a mere 15 miles south of the town of Mendocino, and just one mile south of the village of Elk.

From the Bay Area:
Elk is approximately 3 hours from the Golden Gate Bridge. Take Hwy. 101 to Cloverdale (Last Cloverdale Exit - Hwy. 128 West), then Hwy. 128 over to Hwy. 1. The SeaEscape is located on Hwy. 1 six miles south of the junction of Hwy. 128 and Hwy. 1. Allow considerable more time if traveling north on Hwy. 1 from the Bay Area.


About the Area

All have special features to satisfy visitors of the Mendocino coast. The quaint village of Elk adjoins a State beach known for picnicking and driftwood collecting. This picturesque community has restaurants, shops, and spa services. The Elk Garage has AAA towing and is next to the local Elk Grocery Store. While known for abundant fishing, March and April make this a whale-watcher’s paradise. Mendocino is a charming town with art galleries, restaurants and unique shops for browsing. Fort Bragg, just 10 miles north of Mendocino, is home of the Skunk Train where one can enjoy a half-day trip into the beautiful Redwoods. Relax in seclusion with family and friends or have a quiet stay alone at SeaEscape on the Mendocino Coast!
